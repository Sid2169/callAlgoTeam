Customer success / support
Part-time contractor, 10 hrs/week
Part-time
Cost₹8,000–₹15,000/mo
When to hireWhen refund/query volume exceeds 5/day
Tools they runFreshdesk or Notion helpdesk, Stripe disputes
Key metricRefund rate and chargeback rate
Handles all inbound email queries, refund requests, and Stripe dispute responses. Maintains a complaint log. Escalates anything legally sensitive immediately. This role directly protects your payment processor standing — a well-handled refund request never becomes a chargeback. Needs a clear decision tree: what to approve immediately, what to escalate, what to deny.

Part-time

By month 18 you will have 4–5 contractors all requiring coordination, a course platform, an email system, payment processors, a community, and launch logistics. This is a full-time operations job that you currently do alongside content creation and teaching. The ops manager takes over everything except content and product — freeing you to be the educator, not the administrator. Do not hire this role early. It is expensive and wasted before the system is complex enough to need it.
